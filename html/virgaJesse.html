WORK ABOUT CONTACT

VIRGA JESSE FEESTEN

Description

A group project in the bachelor for the seven-yearly Virga Jesse Festivities in Hasselt, Belgium. The organisation opted for an exhibition in hommage to their late graphic designer Rik Rappoort, for which we made the publication. I was responsible for the addendum with stories about Mr. Rappoort from former colleagues, students and friends.
