About

I’m Giel Boogaerts, a 23-year-old graphic designer from Belgium. My main interests are graphic design, photography, and traveling.

I’m especially passionate about typography and book design — a fascination that extends beyond creating to collecting well-designed books.

In contrary to my personality, I aim to stand out with my designs through originality and concept. I believe that functionality should always drive aesthetics, giving each project a deeper sense of purpose and meaning.

I graduated with honors from the International Master of Reading Type & Typography under the guidance of Dr. Prof. Ann Bessemans and the READSEARCH team at PXL-MAD School of Arts. Throughout my master’s and bachelor’s studies, I developed a strong passion for book design, which became the focus of my master’s project.

Currently, I am continuing to deepen my expertise in typography through the Typography & Book program, while also expanding my skill set with a study in Digital Design. T&B I chose for understanding more about the making and producing of books. As much as the practical and theoretical side of it. I am doing Digital Design to broaden my field beyond printing and to be a more whole designer.
