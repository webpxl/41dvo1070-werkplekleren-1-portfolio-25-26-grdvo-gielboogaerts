WORK ABOUT CONTACT

TYPE-INTER-PLAY

Description

As a new way of navigation within the book between the content table, keyword index, image index and the body text I worked outside the book. I connected referrals within the body text with their respective index through angular lines. Each index has their own color, which are CMYK as the content of the book is about printing machinery. Tracking an image, keyword or chapter from their index to the body text works through the outside of the book, where the 'rectangle' on the side of the book is filled up until the page of the referral. The alignment and size of the body text makes that it perfectly aligns with the lines from the image index.

Requirements

  • Size: A4
  • Font size: max. 3
  • Font weight: 1
  • Font style: 1
  • Use of icons for images
  • Use of 15 related keywords
