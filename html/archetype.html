WORK ABOUT CONTACT

ARCHETYPE

Description

A story/article was given as content and we needed to find a way to structure them through a system. I chose to orden all words by looking at their individual search requests on Google through Google Trends for a specific moment. Then I ranked them on a first level for most searched and then the amount of letters. Each word appears the same amount as it appears in the story/article.

Requirements

  • Size book: max. A4
  • Typeface: 1
  • Type size: 1
  • No images
