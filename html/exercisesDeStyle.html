EXERCISES DE STYLE

Description

A poster based on one of the stories of Raymond Queneaus' "Exercises de style". Here I grouped every kind of word (noun, adjective, verb...) and gave them each a distinct typeface. Then I placed the words in a scrabble-like composition and where each different group overlaps, a new typeface is shown. This was determined based on the combination of groups.

Requirements

  • Size: A2
  • Color: 1 accent color
  • No images
