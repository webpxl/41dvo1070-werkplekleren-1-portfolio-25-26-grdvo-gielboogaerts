MICROTYPE

Description

We were to make a poster and book from the content of an article from the Dutch type designer Martin Majoor. I chose to make initials for each chapter, enlarge them and place them half outside of the page. The other half is placed on the poster so each chapter is physically linked through the initials. The front side of the poster has full initials while the backside as hollow ones.

Requirements

  • Size book: A5
  • Size poster: A3
  • Color use: black & white
  • Content booklet: main content
  • Content poster: extra information in the margins
  • Poster is foldable into a booklet
  • Poster and book are linked through design
